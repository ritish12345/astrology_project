Know About Astrology

Astrology is an ancient practice dating back millennia, claiming to forecast events and understand personality based on celestial bodies' positions. It uses the zodiac - a 12-sign system based on the Earth's apparent path around the Sun - to link earthly affairs with the cosmos. Each sign is attributed to specific traits, influencing individuals born within its timeframe. Though scientifically debunked, astrology holds cultural significance for many, providing entertainment, self-reflection, and a sense of connection to the universe's vastness. So, while not definitive science, its enduring popularity underscores the human desire for meaning and guidance from the skies above.
